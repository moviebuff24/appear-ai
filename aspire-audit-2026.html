Top 3 Quick Wins

1

Build a review ask program — get to 200 reviews in 90 days

Nakisher's 192 reviews is the single reason they rank #1 on ChatGPT and Perplexity over Aspire. You are 44 reviews away from matching that benchmark. A post-appointment SMS with a direct Google review link — sent within 24 hours of a visit — converting just 3 patients per week puts you past 200 in under 15 weeks. At 200+ reviews with a 5.0 rating, Aspire becomes the strongest signal in the Farmington Hills market. This is the highest-leverage action available.

Highest impact
2

Add JSON-LD schema markup to aspire-ortho.com

No LocalBusiness or MedicalBusiness structured data was found on the Aspire website. This is why Perplexity listed only 12 reviews instead of 148 — it couldn't reliably connect the website to the Google Business Profile. A single JSON-LD block added to your homepage's <head> takes a developer 30 minutes and explicitly tells every AI platform: this is the business name, address, phone, hours, services, and review source. Fixes the Perplexity data gap and improves citation accuracy across all platforms.

Highest impact
3

Claim and complete your Healthgrades profile

Jusino Orthodontics has fewer Google reviews than Aspire but ranks #1–2 on ChatGPT and Perplexity for general queries. The reason: their Healthgrades presence. Aspire does not appear in Healthgrades' top 20 display for Farmington Hills orthodontists. Perplexity and ChatGPT both pull from Healthgrades as a primary healthcare source. Claiming the profile and completing it — photo, bio, services, hours — shifts Aspire from invisible to visible on this platform within 4–6 weeks. Free to do; no ongoing cost.
